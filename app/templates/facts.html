Myths

  • The new coronavirus cannot be transmitted in areas with hot and humid climates.

  • Spraying alcohol or chlorine all over your body can kill the new coronavirus if already infected .

  • Vaccines against pneumonia can protect you against the new coronavirus and can be used.

  • The new coronavirus can be transmitted through mosquito bites also.

  • The new coronavirus affects only older people.

Facts

  • The COVID-19 virus can be transmitted in ALL AREAS, including areas with hot and humid weather.

  • Spraying alcohol or chlorine all over your body will not kill viruses that have already entered your body.

  • The virus is so new and different that it needs its own vaccine. Researchers are trying to develop a vaccine against COVID-19

  • No evidence yet that the new coronavirus could be transmitted by mosquitoes

  • People of ALL AGES can be infected by the new coronavirus.
